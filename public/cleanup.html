"Clean up Your Community" Project

Background

LitterBug is a mobile app in early development. Born from the ANU TechLauncher program and commissioned by Deloitte Digital, it aims to tackle the challenge of efficient resource allocation for litter cleanup. Incomplete and inaccurate litter location data often hampers these efforts, prompting LitterBug an app that utilises crowdsourcing and gamification to solve this issue all while involving young people in cleaning their community.
In the first semester, we conducted primary user research and secondary research. This informed our understanding of stakeholder perspectives, problem intricacies, competitor insights, and user needs. During the second semester we successfully implemented an MVP, allowing users to report litter, view locations on a map, and compete on leaderboards.
